For about eight months I took a pause from web work to found an indie game company. I worked about 8 hours every day focusing on game development with C#, Python, TypeScript, Rails, and Unity3D. I directed small teams of artists, composers, and other engineers to build competition-entry games such as the award-winning [Frog Daddy's Dash](https://gwelkind.itch.io/frog-daddys-dash).

Most of my time on this venture went towards a large, long-term passion-project which we have not publicly released information about. You can check out some of my smaller game projects on [my itch page](https://gwelkind.itch.io/).
